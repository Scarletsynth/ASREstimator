JUPITER DAO ASR Rewards Estimator

This rewards calculator provides estimations based on data inputted by users. Please note that it is impossible for the calculator to be entirely precise due to the uncertainty surrounding the remaining number of proposals, and votes to be casted in each, until the end of June. While we strive to provide accurate estimations, and the data is updated after every new vote, factors such as the number of proposals and votes casted in each may fluctuate, impacting the final rewards distribution. Users should use the calculator as a guide and understand that actual rewards may vary.
